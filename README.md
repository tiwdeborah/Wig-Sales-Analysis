# Wig Retail Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis for a Wig Business

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales and customer purchasing behavior for a wig and beauty accessories business. The analysis was conducted to identify revenue trends, customer preferences, product performance, and opportunities for business growth using data analytics tools and dashboard reporting.

## Objectives
•	Analyze sales performance across products and customer segments.

•	Identify top-selling wig categories and revenue drivers.

•	Evaluate customer purchasing behavior and retention patterns.

•	Visualize business performance using interactive dashboards.

## Tools Used
•	Microsoft Excel
•	Microsoft Power BI

## Key Insights
•	Bone Straight wigs generated the highest sales revenue.

•	Jerry curl was the best selling wig (81 orders).

•	Returning customers recorded higher average spending.

•	Social media channels contributed significantly to visibility and sales.

•	Faster delivery timelines were associated with better customer ratings.

Deliverables
•	Excel data cleaning and analysis.

•	Interactive Power BI dashboard.

•	Business performance insights.

•	Sales trend visualizations.
